Helm

 

I guess that the helm layout could consist of not only the various electronical devices and displays but also what is behind the scenes, the furniture layout etc. some upholstery will be involved and generally its where a lot of things converge.

Sightlines

One of the goals for the helm is to have clear and easy sightlines for driving the boat while it is raining. Some of these older cabin cruiser designs had a raised platform in the back for the helmsman and it wouldnt be as protected from the rain but you can see clear advantages for clear weather.

Equipment Locations

Where will the various pieces of equipment be located? Should everything be deep in the cabin or located closer to the helm equipment? Where should fuses and easy access electrical stuff be located?

Materials and waterproofing

Some vinyl can be used on the dash but much of it will probably need to be wood or fiberglass a general mold might need to be created.

 

Current Thoughts

So far the helm is to be centered around the Raymarine® GPM400 System. I have selected a Nauticomp 15" Display and that will take up most of the helm. I am starting to appreciate analog instruments and I may simply integrate more of those.
