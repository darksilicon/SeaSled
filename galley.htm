Galley

 

This is the galley page.

Some of the initial ideas that I had for the galley are as follows.

  • I wanted it to be as functional as possible for a small vessel.
  • Be stable for cooking under docked conditions at a Marina.
  • Have the basics and a few selected extras
  • I have read that the ideal place for a galley is amidships so that rocking is minimal.

 

 

Items I think are important.

  1. Spice Rack
  2. Paper Towels / Holder
  3. Utensils for 4 People.
  4. Coffee Cups
  5. Dinner Plates for 4 People.
  6. A Dinner Table / Work Table / Office Table for a Laptop.
  7. I like the "idea" of nets or things to hold fresh Onions and Produce to cook with.
  8. This is likely a fantasy but it would be lovely to shoe-horn a solid fuel stove into the boat. (I live in florida and this is more of a romantic notion.)
  9. Adequate storage to put away things.
  10. Electric Grill for use while at the dock.
  11. Running Water / Esp. with the option to use shore-water. (Tank-age is limited.)
  12. Sink
  13. Food Storage Space (Pantry / Larder / Stowage)
  14. Task Lighting
  15. Dish Rack / Drainage Area
  16. Trash Can / Trash Storage
  17. Refrigeration
  18. Ice Hold for Short Term Trips with Marina Ice.(no electricity usage)
  19. Wooden Cooking Utensils
  20. Aluminum Foil
  21. Ziploc Bags (Quart and Gallon)
  22. A suitable collection of Nautical Adult Beverages
