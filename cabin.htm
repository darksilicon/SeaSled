The Galley, Interior Upholstery

The cabin encompasses the covered area at the front of the boat and that encompasses the galley and the interior upholstery. I am not sure exactly what boat this picture is from but it has everything stowed nicely behind cabinetry and veneer and this is much more the look that I would like to try and emulate.

A place for everything and everything in its place to whatever extent possible. However I would like to add some decorative touches to make it a bit more unique.
