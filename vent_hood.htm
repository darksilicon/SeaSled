Balanced Operation

Intake air and exhaust air need to be balanced for good ventilation. So two fans running simultaneously for the small cabin. Fans seem to come in two varietys bulkhead mounted and remote mounted.

Exhaust Damper

You don't want to feed a fire unlimited oxygen so most residential installations seem to have a sort of damper system installed. Not sure how this would work in a marine setup but planning on investigating this.
