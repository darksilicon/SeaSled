Upholstery

 

Obviously part of what makes a cruiser an especially pleasing or successful design is one that takes into account the soft furnishings and light control.

I used a "tresco linen" style Sunbrella Fabric as the lining for the curtains to give sun protection and I used a polyester outdoor rated printed fabric for the side you see.

I used V-92 Polyester "SunGard" Thread.
