Adult Beverages Page

 

It wouldn't be a proper yacht without suitable adult beverages so I decided to make a list to capture my ideas in this domain proper gustatory drinks-man-ship.

Buchanan's Pineapple Could be consumed neat. Pineapple seems tropical and exotic and yet scotch goes well with cigars. Small tidy bottle suitable for keeping onboard.
Plantation Rum "Sealander" Dark enough to have some flavor but young enough to mix into cocktails in place of white. Suitably pirate looking and with a pleasingly large cork!

A Blend of three island rums, aged a few years.
