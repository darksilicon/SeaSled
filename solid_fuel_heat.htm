Solid Fuel Heating Stove

 

This is probably the hardest to justify. I first ran across the idea of those sorts of stoves on a youtube video about a British sailor that loved solid fuel stoves. They seem to always work generate a lot of heat and some of them can mount an alcohol burner on top for heating water or food.

I do have to be realistic as its not a very large boat but cold weather on the water does happen and it would be nice to have some warmth. It could be accomodated physically but its really a matter of how much space is being sacrificed for it vs. its utility.
